What was the first thing you paid attention to
when interacting with the experience?

What stood out to me first about this site was the captivating scrolling effects. They was a variety of effects used to activate
and display different pieces of information, and the experience was very engaging. The animation elements were dynamic
and interesting, and I also greatly enjoyed the bold and unique typographic design of the website, as it instantly
established a unique personality and professional impression.

Spend two minutes with the experience and create a list of each of your discrete actions.
  1. I clicked to enable sound on the landing page
  2. I scrolled to unveil more content and progress through the website
  3. I read the typographic information and watched the videos presented as I went
What was the most common action in your two minute interaction with the experience?
My most common action was scrolling, as I was directed on the landing page to do so. There are no obvious alternative user paths available. And once I saw the feedback of more and more information appearing as I scrolled, it became an instinctive behaviour that required no second thought.
